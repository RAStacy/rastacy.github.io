So-Cal Ponds was created in 1999 when the owner Dominic Carone was having a conversation with an old friend about his friends brotherâ€™s pond. Dominic had always loved water, ponds and gardening. After building several ponds he began taking on work for clients and formed Crescent-Canada ponds. After working for several other companies in several different trades Dominic decided to form So-Cal Ponds.

So-Cal Ponds believes in a natural approach to achieving our goals for your pond and surrounding landscape. Often times the symptoms are treated rather than the cause or problem causing the symptoms. By using a natural approach we are able to deliver a balanced ecosystem and a beautiful and enjoyable pond or landscape.

We use only the best materials and supplies available to the pond industry and we stand by our work. With over 12 years in the industry we have seen many products come and go and some stick around for awhile. Those that stick around are the ones we are interested in using in our water features.

We here at So-Cal Ponds cut no corners when it comes to installing or repairing your pond. Cutting a corner now will only guarantee a call back and a dissatisfied client. We believe in doing it right the first time. Our team craves that smile when the client sees the finished product for the first time.

Our company has focused on maintaining koi ponds, water gardens and fountains since its creation. Over the years So-Cal Ponds has expanded its range of work to include pondpond cleaning, irrigation systems, softscape and low voltage lighting. Our crew can design whatever your mind can imagine and make your dreams into realities. So-Cal Ponds has most recently teamed up with an old friend who has her Masters Degree in Landscape architecture, a bachelors of science in environmental horticulture and is a certified Landscape Technician from the California Landscape Contractors Association, to help fine tune the design process.

Over the years Dominic and So-Cal Ponds has worked with many Landscapers, General Contractors, Designers, Businesses and homeowners to help achieve balance in the aquatic garden and surrounding landscape.

We hope to add you to our list of satisfied clients.
