Pond Design Consultation

So-Cal Ponds has been in the business since mid spring of 1999. We are happy to help you design your pond or water garden. We have seen many different styles of ponds, worked with many different brands and styles of pond skimmers, bottom drains, external pond filters and waterfall filters so we have a pretty good idea of what works. We have worked with many different landscape contractors as well as other pond construction companies such as the The Pond Digger.

We are happy to make suggestions for the "do it yourselfer" or design your dream pond for you. A design specialists will come to your location, gather some details such as your expectations of your pond, the location, size, and style of pond before coming up with a design. Often times we are able to give a quick design right there on the spot. Selecting the right pond pump, pond filter and location are some of the most important elements to designing a successful backyard pond.
