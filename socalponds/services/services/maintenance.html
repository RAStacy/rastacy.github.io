Pond Maintenance

No matter who built your pond or what kind of filter was installed your pond is going to need some sort of maintenance. Whether it be a yearly cleaning or weekly maintenance it is going to happen. We have heard many stories where the homeowners were told at the original consultation "all you have to do is this and that once, maybe twice a year." With over 15 years maintaining water features we have NEVER seen a pond or water feature that did not need regular maintenance. We offer twice a month, twice a week (or more) and weekly service with a rare occasion of a monthly visit. Prices vary depending on many factors, like equipment, design, fish load and surrounding landscape.

We service all koi ponds and filtration systems, water gardens and fountains.

So-Cal Ponds staff is knowledgeable and experience with traditional koi pond filtration, settlement chambers, gravel, bead, and sand filters. We are also current with the newer major systems such as AquaScape Designs, Savio, Matala, PondSweep and Helix Life Support systems.

All maintenance visits include the following:

  • Clean and inspection of pump and basket
  • Cleaning of skimmer net/basket and inspection
  • Skimming top and bottom of pond for leaves and debris
  • Trimming and fertilization of aquatic plants
  • Fish health inspection
  • Adding water and any needed additives like beneficial bacteria, dechlorinator or algaecides
  • Check pond, equipment, and streams or waterfall for potential leaks

If you are interested in a free maintenance quote please fill out the form below and a representative will contact you shortly.




Some of the free benefits of weekly service are:

  • Regular removal of debris in skimmers, ponds surface and pump baskets
  • Inspection and replacement of UV lights*
  • Backwashing of filtration system
  • Free delivery of plants, livestock, additives and anything else purchased from So-Cal Ponds
  • Water light maintenance/bulb replacement**
  • Under water light maintenance/bulb replacement**
  • Free 30 minute "Emergency Service Call" every six months

With regular maintenance we can stay ahead of any issues that may arise. Some ponds are designed so well the pond may only need service twice a month.

We stock many items you may need or want for your pond, like additional underwater lights, quality koi food, additives and we grow most of our own aquatic plants and with regular maintenance we are happy to drop these items off on our next visit.

* UV light must be purchased before it is installed

** up to 4 replacement bulbs a year. LED not included
